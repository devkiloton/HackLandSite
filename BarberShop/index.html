About The BarberShop

Located in the heart of the city, The Barbershop brings to the market what's best for your hair and beard. Founded in 2019, The Barbershop is already a highlight in the city and wins new customers every day.

Our mission: "self-esteem and quality of life to the clients".

We offer experienced professionals who are in tune with the changes in the fashion world. The service has a standard of excellence and agility, ensuring quality and satisfaction of our customers.
